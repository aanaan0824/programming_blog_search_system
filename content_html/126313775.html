<1>题目一

题目：运营想要知道复旦大学学生gpa最高值是多少，请你取出相应数据

代码：

select gpa
from
(select gpa,
row_number()over(partition by university order by gpa desc) as ranking
from user_profile
where university = '复旦大学') as t
where t.ranking = 1;
